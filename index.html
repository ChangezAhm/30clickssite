Privacy Policy

30 CLICKS LTD does not collect, store or process your personal data beyond what is necessary to provide you with our service. We only collect your email address for order notifications and delivery updates.

Your photos remain private and are only used to fulfill your print orders. We do not use your photos for any other purpose, and they are deleted from our servers once your order has been processed.
