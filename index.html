About the 30 CLICKS Beta

Beta Test Info: What You're Getting Into

As a beta tester for 30 Clicks, you're getting a limited preview of the full experience - 10 photos (instead of the usual 30), printed and delivered to you for free.

This isn't just a tech test, we want you to experience the whole thing, from the moment you open the app to the moment your photos land in your letter box.

What You'll Be Testing

  • The app: is it smooth, confusing, crap, glitchy, sexy?
  • Delivery & packaging: how does it arrive? Does it feel special? Do you care?
  • Print quality: how do your photos look and feel?

We're looking for open, honest feedback, that's what helps us most.

Extra Thoughts We'd Love

  • How much would you pay for a 30-photo print experience like this?
  • Live reactions while using the app? Send them our way. In the moment insights are gold.
  • Any bugs or crashes? Please report them right away. First, sorry. Second, we'll fix them fast.

Your Data

All your data is safely stored in a secure Firebase cloud database. Nobody wants to see your crap pics anyway (joking)

That's it, have fun and thanks again for helping us build something better.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

Privacy Policy

30 CLICKS LTD does not collect, store or process your personal data beyond what is necessary to provide you with our service. We only collect your email address for order notifications and delivery updates.

Your photos remain private and are only used to fulfill your print orders. We do not use your photos for any other purpose, and they are deleted from our servers once your order has been processed.

We do not share your data with any third parties except for our printing partner who needs your delivery information to fulfill your order.

Terms of Service

By using 30 CLICKS, you agree not to upload any content that is illegal, offensive, or infringes on others' rights. We reserve the right to refuse service for any violations.

Once you've taken 30 photos, your prints will be automatically ordered. Please ensure your delivery address is accurate as we cannot reissue prints for incorrect addresses.

Print delivery typically takes 5-7 business days, though times may vary based on your location. All sales are final and we do not offer refunds except in cases where prints arrive damaged.
